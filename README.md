## How the Partner Value Pack works

1. Open the tool in a browser.
2. Enter your details (your email is where buyer enquiries land) and your partner's business name and brand colour.
3. Tap "Copy shareable link." The link carries your details, so every enquiry comes back to you.
4. Send that link to your referral partner or your clients.

Each broker builds their own link. A link belongs to the broker who created it.
