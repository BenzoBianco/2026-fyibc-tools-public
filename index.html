Three ways to use it
01

Co-brand it with a referral partner

Put your partner's business name and brand colour on the front, with your name and details underneath. Share the link with the partner, an agent, accountant or buyer's agent, so their clients use a tool that looks like the partner's, and every enquiry comes back to you. This is the relationship play: you give the partner something useful instead of asking them for referrals.

02

Run it as your own lead magnet

Put your own business name in the partner spot and your name at the top, so the whole tool sits inside your brand. Now it is a lead magnet that sends enquiries straight to you. Drop the link on your website, in a newsletter, a social post or your email signature, anywhere you want people to check their numbers and reach you directly.

03

Turn your link into a QR code

Take your shareable link and create a free QR code in Canva. Put that code where people already are: a sticker, a flyer, a window display, a business card, an open-home sign, your website. People scan it, complete the tool on their phone, and send their snapshot to you. One link, working in the real world as well as online.
